Vector Map: Historical Castles in Utrecht and Their Reachability

A visualization of castle locations in Utrecht and their biking-time accessibility, created using OpenStreetMap data and OpenRouteService.

Map of Utrecht Castles

About This Map

The process of making this map started by importing data from OpenStreetMap, a free and open map created by volunteers around the world. The castle locations were cleaned and transformed into point data. Using OpenRouteService, biking times from each castle were simulated, revealing that most castles around Utrecht are 5 to 15 minutes apart.

This map is useful for tourism: visitors can explore Dutch heritage by biking between castles, planning itineraries based on travel time rather than direct distance.
